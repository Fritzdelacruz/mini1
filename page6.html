Appointment Setting Services


An Appointment Setting provides pre-sales services from our nearshore facilities to business owners. Basically, the Appointment Setting Virtual Assistant does the “pre-sales” scheduling of consumer and business prospects via outbound telemarketing.

Consequently this helps streamline your company’s sales process, with your more experienced internal and external sales personnel dealing with pre-qualified sales appointments.

A business owner understands that pre-sales appointment scheduling tasks take a lot of time from his/her sales staff. Hence, reducing these pre-sales scheduling tasks allows them to focus on pre-qualified direct sales opportunities. As a result, you can increase your sales conversions resulting in business growth and revenue.

Successful business owners use their more experienced and expensive sales personnel for sales opportunities, by outsourcing preliminary pre-sales scheduling to an Appointment Setting Virtual Assistant. Obviously, it is just a smart business decision.
